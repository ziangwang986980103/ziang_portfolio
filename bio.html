Hi! I am Ziang!
I am a senior student at the University of Michigan, majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Science. My academic journey has been driven by a fascination with the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Mixed Reality, and AI for Education. I'm particularly passionate about how technology can enhance learning experiences and am involved in several research projects in this area. To learn more about my work, please visit my Portfolio page.
Outside the lab and classroom, I'm an avid traveler and nature enthusiast. Some of my most memorable adventures include hiking in Yosemite and Bryce Canyon National Parks. I also find great joy in playing film scores on the piano. To hear some of my favorite pieces, feel free to visit my Music page.
