My Heart Will Go On


"Titanic" has always been one of my favorite movies, with its portrayal of the timeless love between Jack and Rose resonating deeply with me. The theme song, "My Heart Will Go On," is not just widely recognized but also holds a special place in my heart. Playing it on the piano allows me to relive the emotions and memories attached to both the film and the song.


The piano score I've chosen is by David Osborne, whose interpretation captures the essence of the song beautifully. You can find his performance on youtube. For those interested in the technical side, the score is available at Musicnotes. This particular arrangement has some intricate sections that challenge me and parts that I find profoundly enjoyable to play.





Merry Christmas, Mr. Lawrence


The hauntingly beautiful strains of 'Merry Christmas, Mr. Lawrence' first captivated me upon my initial listening, a melody that remains etched in my mind. Composed by the legendary Ryuichi Sakamoto, this piece is not just a testament to his musical genius but also to his indomitable spirit. As a composer and pianist, Sakamoto-san has contributed richly to the world of music with his evocative compositions.

His courage and resilience in the face of illness are profoundly inspiring. Despite battling a tumor, Ryuichi Sakamoto's dedication to his art is unwavering, a true embodiment of the belief that music is a life force. It is with deep respect and a kindred spirit of never yielding to adversity that I present my rendition of this iconic piece.

In playing 'Merry Christmas, Mr. Lawrence,' I strive to channel some of Sakamoto's extraordinary courage and passion. His music is a reminder of the power of perseverance, and I hope to honor his legacy through my performance. May it be a tribute not just to his artistry but to the enduring human spirit that he so remarkably represents.





Merry-go-round of life


I've always been drawn to the music of 'Merry-go-round of Life' because it has the feel of a romantic waltz. It's a piece that takes you on a journey, with a melody that swings you gently back and forth, just like a dance.

I have a deep appreciation for Joe Hisaishi's work, especially his film scores. They have a way of perfectly capturing the emotions of a scene, and 'Merry-go-round of Life' is no exception. Playing Hisaishi's music is always a joy for me, and I hope you'll enjoy listening to it as much as I enjoy bringing it to life on the piano.
