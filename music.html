My Heart Will Go On


"Titanic" is one of my favorite movies; the timeless love between Jack and Rose is truly touching. "My Heart Will Go On," as the theme song of Titanic, is widely known and also one of my favorite songs. I am very happy that I can play it on the piano.


The piano score is from David Osborne. His play can be found at this link: youtube The score is available at Musicnotes if you are interested in it: My heart will go on
